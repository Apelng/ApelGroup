UBA PLC Rights Issue

CardinalStone Partners Limited is pleased to inform you that United Bank for Africa Plc’s (“UBA” or the “Bank”) Rights Issue of 3,156,869,665 ordinary shares of 50 Kobo each at ₦50.00 per share, on the basis of 1 new share for every 13 held as of close of business on July 16, 2025, is NOW OPEN and scheduled to close on September 05, 2025.

Rights Issue Details

  • Issue Price: ₦50.00 per share
  • Qualification Date: July 16, 2025
  • Offer Period: July 30 – September 05, 2025
  • Equity Shelf Programme: Tranche II under ₦400 billion shelf
