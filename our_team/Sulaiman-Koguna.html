Our Team

Meet Our Directors

Sulaiman Koguna

Director, Apel Group

Professional Background

Sulaiman Koguna is a financial services expert with over a decade of experience in insurance, finance, and management. As a Director at Apel, he contributes strategic insights to drive the organization’s growth and operational excellence.

Career Highlights

  • Executive Director at Koguna Babura Insurance Brokers Limited.
  • Founder of e-Insurance Solution Center, a digital insurance platform.
  • Specializes in risk management and insurance product development.
  • Holds a BSc in Economics and postgraduate diploma in Finance.

Key Achievements

Sulaiman has been pivotal in modernizing insurance services and expanding access to financial protection for individuals and businesses.

Notable Contributions

  • Launched innovative micro-insurance products for underserved markets.
  • Developed digital tools that increased insurance penetration by 25%.
  • Advised corporate clients on enterprise risk management strategies.
  • Member of the Chartered Insurance Institute of Nigeria (CIIN).
