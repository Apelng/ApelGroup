Our Team

Meet Our Management

Peter Aletor

Group Managing Director/Chief Executive Officer

Professional Background

Peter Aletor is a professional accountant and a pioneer staff of Capital Express Securities Limited, where he rose to become the company’s Managing Director. With over 30 years of experience in the financial services industry, he has established himself as a leading figure in the Nigerian capital market.

Career Highlights

  • Founder and Group Managing Director of Apel, overseeing the entire organization.
  • Established Apel Asset Limited in 2008, specializing in big-ticket transactions for individual and corporate clients.
  • Authorized Dealing Clerk of The Nigerian Exchange Group (NGX).
  • Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Stockbrokers (CIS).

Key Achievements

Peter Aletor has been instrumental in driving the growth and success of Apel, transforming it into a leading financial services group in Nigeria. His leadership has been pivotal in executing high-value transactions and expanding the company’s market presence.

Notable Contributions

  • Spearheaded the establishment of Apel Asset Limited, which has become a key player in the Nigerian capital market.
  • Played a critical role in the execution of major mergers, acquisitions, and capital raising transactions.
  • Mentored and developed a team of skilled professionals in the financial services industry.
  • Alumnus of Lagos Business School (LBS) and Harvard Business School’s Reimagining Strategy Course.
