Adetoro Ojelabi

Head, Legal & Company Secretary

Professional Background

Adetoro has over 15 years of experience across Banking, Litigation, Company Secretaryship, Legal Drafting, Compliance, Human Resources, and Administration. She is an astute lawyer with a Bachelor of Laws degree, LL.B. from Olabisi Onabanjo University, Ogun State, and a Master of Laws degree LL.M. from the University of London, United Kingdom. She is also a member of the Chartered Institute of Secretaries & Administrators, the Institute of Chartered Mediators and Conciliators, and the Compliance Institute, Nigeria. She began her career at Oceanic Bank International Plc and thereafter moved to Ecobank Nigeria Limited. She is the Company Secretary and Chief Compliance Officer of Apel.
