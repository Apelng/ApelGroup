Job Information

Sales & Deal Origination

  • Investment Banking
  • Onsite
  • Lagos, Nigeria

Job Summary:

We are seeking an experienced Business Development Manager to lead our efforts in penetrating new markets and governments. The successful candidate will have a proven track record of driving business growth, building strategic relationships, and identifying new opportunities.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and execute business development strategies to penetrate new markets and governments
  • Identify and pursue new business opportunities, including partnerships, collaborations, and tenders
  • Conduct market research and analysis to identify trends, opportunities, and challenges
  • Collaborate with internal teams, including sales, marketing, and product development, to drive business growth
  • Develop and present pitches, proposals, and presentations to potential clients and partners

Requirements:

  • Minimum 10 years of experience in business development, sales, or a related field
  • Proven track record of driving business growth and penetrating new markets and governments
  • Excellent communication, negotiation, and presentation skills
  • Strong network of contacts in government, industry, and business
  • Strategic thinker with the ability to analyse market trends and identify opportunities
  • Strong leadership and team management skills

What We Offer:

  • Competitive salary and benefits package
  • Opportunity to work with a dynamic and growing organisation
  • Excellent communication, negotiation, and presentation skills
  • Collaborative and supportive team environment
  • Professional development and growth opportunities

How to Apply:

If you are a motivated and experienced business development professional looking for a new challenge, please submit your application, including your resume and cover letter, to careers@apelasset.com
